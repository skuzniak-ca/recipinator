Save Recipes from Your Browser

Add a bookmarklet to your browser so you can save recipes while browsing, or use the quick-add page on mobile.

On Mobile?

Mobile browsers don't support bookmarklets, but you can still save recipes in a few quick steps:

1
While on a recipe page, copy the URL from your browser's address bar.
2
Open the quick-add page and paste the URL.
3
Tap "Add Recipe" and you're done.
Tip: Add the quick-add page to your home screen for fast access. In your browser's menu, look for "Add to Home Screen" or "Install".
